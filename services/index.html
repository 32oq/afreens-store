Common questions

Get clarity on process, timeline, and customization before placing your bespoke calligraphy request.

How do I place a custom order?

Use the contact form or WhatsApp button to share your vision, and Afreen will reply with a custom consultation and timeline.

What is the typical production time?

Most projects require 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and finishing options. Larger bespoke commissions may require additional time.

Can I request revisions?

Yes, revisions are part of the design process for stationery and branding projects to ensure the final artwork is precisely tailored.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, premium packaging and trusted courier services are available for domestic and international delivery.
